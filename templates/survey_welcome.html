Welcome to this Study

This study is interested in how people make investment decisions with the assistance of artificial intelligence (AI).

⏱
Duration
15 to 20 minutes
🔄
Rounds
15 investment rounds
💰
Task
Invest $1,000 per round

🏆 Instructions, Rules, and Bonus Prize

You will make 15 rounds of investment decisions. Please read the instructions and make your decisions carefully at each step of your way. Each round will follow a similar process.

Imagine you are entitled to investing $1,000 "seed money" in each round. Your decisions in each round will influence your "final return" (i.e., profit or loss) at the end of the 15 rounds. Your cumulative balance at the end of the 15 rounds minus the $1,000 × 15 = $15,000 seed money will be your final return.

🎧 How to win a bonus prize:
If your final return places you in the top 10% of all participants, congratulations, you will earn a chance to win a new Apple AirPods 4 through a random drawing.

Please do not use the browser back button at any point during this study. Use only the Next buttons provided. Back navigation is monitored and may result in disqualification and forfeiture of rewards and bonuses.

Your participation is voluntary and your responses are completely anonymous. Data will be used for academic research purposes only.
